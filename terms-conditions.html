Understanding how we manage your experience

Website Cookie Policy

Baby Memorial Hospital (BABY MEMORIAL HOSPITAL/“Us”/“We”) understand that your privacy is important to you and are committed to being transparent about the technologies it uses. We believe in being transparent about how we collect, and use data related to you. For more information, please read our Privacy Policy.

This Cookie Policy explains how and why cookies may be stored on and accessed from your device when you use or visit our website or app that posts a link to this policy (collectively, our "Sites"). By clicking "Accept" on the cookie banner, you give us your consent to store and access cookies and other tracking technologies as described in this Cookie Policy. This Cookie Policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files of information that a web server generates and sends to a web browser. Web browsers store the cookies they receive for a predetermined period of time, or for the length of a user's session on a website. They attach the relevant cookies to any future requests the user makes of the web server. It allows the website to recognize your device and store some information about your preferences or past actions.

There are different types of cookies. Some cookies come directly from our website and others come from third parties which place cookies on our site.

Essential / Strictly Necessary Cookies
  • Authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts.
  • Maintain session integrity as you navigate through different pages.
  • Facilitate the completion of transactions and form submissions.
Non-Essential Cookies

Non-essential cookies are used to enhance your browsing experience and provide additional functionality that is not strictly necessary for the basic operation of our website. These cookies help us understand your preferences, enable personalized content, and improve our marketing efforts. While these cookies enhance your experience, they are not required for the website to function. You can choose to accept or decline these cookies through your browser settings or cookie preference link.

Performance Cookies
  • Measure and improve the performance of our website.
  • Collect statistical data on website usage (e.g., page load times, visitor numbers).
  • Identify and fix issues to enhance user experience.
Functionality & Profile Cookies
  • Remember your login details and keep you logged in during your visit.
  • Store your language preferences or region selection.
  • Provide enhanced features like personalized content and recommendations.
  • Enable live chat support and other interactive services.
Targeting Cookies

Targeting or marketing cookies are used to present you with content and updates that are relevant to your interests, particularly related to our healthcare services and wellness initiatives. These cookies help us understand your browsing patterns on our website and may also collect information about your interactions with third-party websites. These cookies may be set by Baby Memorial Hospital or by authorized third-party service providers who support our digital communications.

  • Measure the effectiveness of awareness or service-related campaigns.
  • Assess the reach and effectiveness of our health awareness and promotional campaigns.
  • Limit repetitive content to enhance your browsing experience.
  • Collaborate with trusted partners to deliver more meaningful and relevant information through online platforms.

The collection and processing of personal data through such cookies are done only with your consent, which can be withdrawn at any time. Any information shared—such as a pseudonymized identifier like an encrypted email or device ID—is handled with strict safeguards and is used solely for the purpose of providing relevant, responsible communication.

Your privacy and trust are important to us. We ensure that your data is used fairly, transparently, and only for the purposes you have agreed to.

What we use cookies for?

We use cookies and similar technologies to recognize your browser and identify you as logged in to our website. This ensures you connect to the right service when changes are made to the website. Cookies help tailor the website and our products to suit your interests, needs, and preferences, enabling smooth navigation and feature usage. They also collect information on how you use the website, providing statistics and helping us improve by measuring any errors. Additionally, cookies provide advertising agencies with information about your visit, allowing them to present you with advertisements that may interest you.

For How Long We Store Cookies?

Cookies can be stored on your browser or device for varying lengths of time. Temporary or session cookies are deleted from your computer or device when you close your web browser. Persistent or permanent cookies will remain stored on your computer or device until they are deleted or until they reach their expiry date.

Keeping your information safe

We are committed to protecting your privacy and do not store any personal information such as your name, address, phone number or email in the cookies we use. Our cookies cannot read or search your computer, smartphone or web-enabled device to obtain information about you or your family, or read any material kept on your hard drive.

By virtue of using the website and changing your browser settings to “accepting cookies”, you consent to the use of the cookies by BABY MEMORIAL HOSPITAL in accordance with this Cookie Policy.

Once you have given your consent, we will place a cookie on your device to remember your consent for future visits to our site. This cookie will expire periodically. If you wish to withdraw your consent at any time, you will need to delete your cookies using your internet browser settings.

You have the option to block the cookies by adjusting your browser settings. However, if the cookies are disabled, it will have an impact on your overall experience while using our website.

How do you change cookie preferences?

Within your browser, you have the option to manage your cookie preferences. Different browsers provide various controls to help you decide whether to accept cookies. Typically, your browser will offer you the choice to accept, refuse or delete cookies at all times, or those from providers that website owners use i.e. third-party cookies, or those from specific websites. Each browser’s website should contain instructions on how you can do this.

For further information please refer to: Cookie Preference link.

If you choose to block cookies on our website, please note that you may be unable to access certain areas, and some functionalities and pages may not work as intended.

If you wish to remove previously stored cookies, you can manually delete them at any time. However, this will not prevent our site from placing new cookies on your device unless you adjust your internet browser settings as described above.
