Overview

About BMH Perumbavoor

BMH Perumbavoor is a modern multi-speciality healthcare destination committed to delivering high-quality medical care with compassion, innovation, and clinical excellence. Designed to serve the growing healthcare needs of Perumbavoor and surrounding communities, the hospital combines advanced medical infrastructure with patient-centered care.

With a strong focus on safety, comfort, and medical excellence, BMH Perumbavoor offers comprehensive healthcare services across key specialties supported by experienced doctors, trained nursing professionals, and advanced diagnostic facilities. The hospital is built to ensure faster access to quality healthcare while maintaining the trusted standards and legacy associated with the BMH healthcare network.

Transit & Directions

How to Reach BMH

Conveniently situated in the heart of Perumbavoor, our medical campus is easily accessible through multiple transportation networks across Kerala.

By Air

Cochin International Airport (COK)

Located just a short drive away. Readily active pre-paid taxi booths and direct airport feeder lines ensure speedy commutes.

12 km • 20 Mins Drive

By Rail

Aluva Railway Station

The nearest major railhead connecting all major Indian cities. Active auto stands and local cabs are stationed just outside the station.

15 km • 25 Mins Drive

By Road

Perumbavoor KSRTC Stand

Sits right along the Main Central (MC) Road. Easily accessible via city buses, inter-district coaches, and private transport lines.

1.5 km • 5 Mins Drive

By Metro

Aluva Metro Station

Direct connection to the entire Kochi Metro corridor. Direct feeder services, app-cabs, and local shuttles run continuously to Perumbavoor.

14.5 km • 22 Mins Drive
